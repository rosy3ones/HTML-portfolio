Best Movies According to Rosemary

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Arrival

This is an amazing movie that deals with a character's decision to go down a path that she already knows will hurt her, just because it also meant so much to her.

Mr. Nobody

The movie follows a man down multiple choices he's made in life. In the end, it didn't matter what he chose because everything turned out okay each way.

Buried

This movie is one of the best, if not the best, movie that Ryan Reynolds has ever made because it gives him so much opportunity to act through a wide range of emotions with very little tools.
